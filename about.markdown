# About Tarsis Lima (@tarsislimadev)

Welcome to the official blog of **Tarsis Lima**, also known online as **@tarsislimadev**.  
This space is dedicated to sharing insights, experiences, and projects around software development, technology, and the journey of continuous learning.

## Who I Am

I’m a developer passionate about building solutions that make a difference. My work spans across web development, open-source contributions, and exploring new tools and frameworks that push the boundaries of what’s possible.

## What You’ll Find Here

On this blog, I write about:
- Practical tutorials and guides for developers
- Thoughts on programming languages, frameworks, and tools
- Personal projects and experiments
- Reflections on career growth and the developer community

## Why This Blog

I believe knowledge grows when it’s shared. This blog is my way of documenting my journey, giving back to the community, and connecting with fellow developers who share the same curiosity and drive.
